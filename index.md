# Introduction to Computational Linguistics

*A book-oriented presentation of Computational Linguistics, emphasizing linguistic intuition, clarity, and accessibility.*

---

## About This Book

This website is part of the book  
**_Introduction to Computational Linguistics_**  
by **Muhammad Shoaib Tahir**.

The material is designed for readers from linguistics and related disciplines who wish to understand computational approaches to language **without heavy mathematical formalism**. The focus is on intuition, conceptual clarity, and practical understanding.

---

## Scope and Philosophy

The chapters emphasize:

- Linguistic intuition over formal derivation  
- Conceptual explanations of probabilistic and computational models  
- Visual reasoning through figures and diagrams  
- Accessibility for non-mathematical readers  

---
